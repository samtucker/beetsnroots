WE ARE TRUE TO OUR ROOTS...

VG: 99% of our food is plant based (except for added honey, eggs and cheese where specified as additional extras) We believe a plant based diet brings us all a little closer to maintaining a healthy body and a sustainable planet. The milk we use is coconut, oat and almond.

GF: Everything we serve is prepared with Gluten free ingredients (including our stocks and tamari) except wraps, sourdough bread and some granolas. Please just ask to replace the bread with our delicious seeded GF bread, and specify GF granola. Unfortunately we have not sourced gluten free wraps that are good enough, but we are working on it!

ORGANIC:We use organic ingredients as much as possible, occasionally we may have to substitute where necessary.

ECO: We care very much about our planet and use eco and compostable packaging for our takeaways, we also encourage bringing in your own re usable containers; in fact we even offer you 10% when you do!

Lunch is served from 11:30am until close.
